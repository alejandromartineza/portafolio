2 de febrero dia de la marmota

Groundhog Day is a very special day for some communities in the United States where the end of winter and the beginning of spring are marked thanks to the small rodent we know as a groundhog,which means that if the groundhog sees its shadow the winter will stretch a little more otherwise the beginning of spring occurs.
